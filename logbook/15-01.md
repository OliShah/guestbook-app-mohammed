### Manifests
_Backend Deployment:_
The backend deployment provides the blueprint for the backend pods. It will:
	- ensure 2 identical pods are running at all times 
	- provide resource limits to the container
	- inject env variables, config maps and secrets into container
	- define container's networking and service access
